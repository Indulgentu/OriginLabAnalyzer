Overview
An introduction to OLA and the basics to get you started.
Introduction

The digital era has brought us a huge advantage: if you are too lazy to do something by hand, just tell the computer how to do it and it will!

As such, the aim of OLA is to automatize the process of data importing/analyzing in OriginLab.

About

OLA was custom made for Technische Universität Ilmenau as part of the Erasmus+ traineeship program, to automatize the importing of data into OriginLab.

OLA has two main features: parsing data from CSV/Parsed .txt files and automatically generating tables and graphs into OriginLab.

Get started

In order to start using OLA, you must have OriginLab 9.5 or later versions installed.
