Analyzing
Generating tables and graphs based on parsed data.
About

To generate the tables and graphs, you have to provide OLA with the parsed .txt files.

How to Analyze

Follow the following steps to analyze:

1. Pick the parsed files. You can either pick the directory (OLA will automatically add all parsed files from within that directory) or manually choose them/drag and drop them into the list.

2. When done, click Analyze...

3. Files will now be read and OLA will generate the tables and graphs. Might take a while until OriginLab shows, this is normal.
